# Index Laws
### Index Law 1:
$a^m \times a^n = a^{m+n}$
### Index Law 2:
$$\frac {a^m} {a^n} = a^{m-n}$$
### Index Law 3:
$$(a^m)^n = a^{mn}$$
### Zero Power:
$$a^0 = 1$$ <center>where</center> $$a \ne 0$$
### Index Law 4:
$$(ab)^m = a^mb^m$$
### Index Law 5:
$$\bigg( \frac{a}{b}\bigg) ^ m = \frac{a^m}{b^m}$$<center>where</center> $$b \ne 0$$
